1. PATIENT: Stores general patient information.		(16)
   - PatientID (Primary Key) 				- Integer(4)		Auto_Increment
   - FirstName					- Varchar(20)
   - LastName					- Varchar(15)
   - DateOfBirth					- Date
   - Gender					- Char(1)			M/F/O
   - ContactNumber					- Varchar(15)
   - Email						- Varchar(30)
   - Address					- Varchar(50)
   - City						- Varchar(20)
   - Insured					- Char(1)			Y/N
   - InsuranceCompany				- Varchar(50)
   - ReferredBy					- Varchar(50)
   - FirstVisitDate					- Date
  ***REMOVE - LastVisitDate					- Date
   - AllergicTo					- Varchar(30)
   - History						- Varchar(250)

2. HISTORY: Records patient's medical history.		(08)
   - HistoryID (Primary Key)				- Integer(5)		Auto_Increment
   - VisitID (Foreign Key - VISIT)				- Integer(5)
   - Diagnosis					- Varchar(50)		
   - DiagnosisDate					- Date
   - Observation					- Varchar(100)
   - Treatment					- Varchar(100)
   - LabTest					- Varchar(50)
   - Notes						- Varchar(100)

3. DOCTOR: Contains information about doctors.		(07)
   - DoctorID (Primary Key)				- Integer(3)		Auto_Increment
   - FirstName					- Varchar(20)
   - LastName					- Varchar(15)
   - Specialization					- Varchar(30)
   - ContactNumber					- Varchar(15)
   - Email						- Varchar(30)
   - Availability					- Varchar(50)

4. PRESCRIPTION: Manages prescription details.		(04)
   - PrescriptionID (Primary Key)			- Integer(5)		Auto_Increment
   - HistoryID (Foreign Key - HISTORY)			- Integer(5)
   - Medicine					- Varchar(250)
   - Instruction					- Varchar(250)

5. VISIT: Logs patient visits and consultations.		(08)
   - VisitID (Primary Key)				- Integer(5)		Auto_Increment
   - PatientID (Foreign Key - PATIENT)			- Integer(4)
   - DoctorID (Foreign Key - DOCTOR)			- Integer(3)
   - VisitDateTime					- TimeStamp		Default Current_Timestamp
   - CaseFee					- Integer(3)
   - VisitCategory 					- Char(1)			S/E/C/R/O
   - CaseFeeType (New, Renew, Special, Consult, Other)		- Char(1)			N/R/S/C/O
						(Scheduled Visit, Emergency, Consultation, Referred Visit, Other)	
   - Notes						- Varchar(20)
